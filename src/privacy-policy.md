# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your data when you use our website. We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your data is protected.

## 2. Types of Data Collected
We use device fingerprinting to collect technical information about your device, such as browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution, installed fonts, plugins, and other attributes.

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection
We collect this data for security and functionality purposes, including:

Detecting suspicious behavior
Preventing unauthorized access
Improving website performance and user experience
## 4. Legal Basis
We collect this data based on your explicit consent. You may provide or withdraw consent at any time.

## 5. Consent
You have the option to give or decline consent for fingerprinting. If you decline consent, we will not collect any fingerprinting data.

## 6. Revocation of Consent
You can revoke your consent at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will remove all data associated with your device.

## 7. Data Storage and Security
The fingerprinting data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized administrators. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

## 8. Data Retention
We retain the fingerprinting data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or until you revoke your consent.
